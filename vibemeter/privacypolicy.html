To provide our AI analysis, you upload video files to our system. We do not permanently store your video content. Once the analysis is complete and the "Virality Score" is returned to your device, the video data is discarded from our processing servers.

We may collect anonymous usage data (device type, crash logs, feature usage) to improve the app stability.

Please note that this app is a tool for creators and is not associated with TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube. We do not collect your login credentials for these social platforms, nor do we access your private data on those platforms.

4. Data Security

5. Children's Privacy
