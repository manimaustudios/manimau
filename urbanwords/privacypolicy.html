Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 07, 2025

Manimai Studios ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Urban Words mobile game (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Game. By using the Game, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide and improve the Game. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized data about how you interact with the Game, such as session duration, features used, and device information (e.g., device type, operating system version).
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads, including advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID), IP address, and approximate location based on your device settings.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or precise location unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through support inquiries).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game.
  • Analyze usage trends to improve the Game’s functionality and user experience.
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics: For usage analytics. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: For in-game advertisements. See Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob as described above.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@manimaustudios.com.
